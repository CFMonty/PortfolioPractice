Case Study: USDA Redesign

The goal of this project was to redesign the US Department of Agriculture (USDA) website, focusing on the user interface, navigation, and information architecture. Founded in 1862 and signed into law by President Lincoln, the USDA provides leadership on food, agriculture, natural resources, and related issues based on public policy, the best available science, and effective management. Over the course of 4 weeks our team completed a responsive hi-fidelity redesign of the website and its navigation.
